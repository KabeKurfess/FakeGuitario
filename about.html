Meet The Team

Our team at Guitario all play the guitar in some way or another. We all love to connect with students if they need any help. At Guitario we make it our priority to have a welcoming enviorment for everyone to enjoy and learn. Here you can meet the whole team we have at Guitario!

Jimmy Page

JamesPage is an English musician, songwriter, multi-instrumentalist and record producer who achieved international success as the guitarist and founder of the rock band Led Zeppelin.

Carlos Santana

Carlos Santana is an American guitarist who rose to fame in the late 1960s and early 1970s with his band Santana.

Bob Marley

Bob Marley was a Jamaican singer, songwriter, and musician. Considered one of the pioneers of reggae, his musical career was marked by fusing elements of reggae, ska, and rocksteady, as well as his distinctive vocal and songwriting style.

Eddie Van halen

Eddie Van Halen was an American musician and songwriter. He was the main songwriter, guitarist and keyboardist of the American rock band Van Halen, which he co-founded in 1972 with his brother, drummer Alex Van Halen, bassist Mark Stone, and singer David Lee Roth.

Tony Iommi

Tony Iommi is a British guitarist. He was the guitarist, co-founder and leader of heavy metal band Black Sabbath, and was the band's primary composer and sole continuous member for nearly five decades.

Lindsey Buckingham

Lindsey Buckingham is an American singer, musician, songwriter, and producer, best known as the former lead guitarist and male lead singer of the music group Fleetwood Mac from 1975 to 1987 and 1997 to 2018.

The Courses We Offer

At Guitario We offer two general categories of courses. One being for acoustic guitar and one being for an electric guitar. Total we have 18 different courses to choose from each of them ranging from levels 1-9. The further up the level you go the more experience you will need to take the course. Don't worry, take your time. There is no time limit here at Guitario we want you to move at your own pace. We think everyone can have a chance at learning to play the guitar which is why we have set no real paramaters. Visit the contact page and mark which course you would like to start on to get signed up now!
